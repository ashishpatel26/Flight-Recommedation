Dataset:
The data contains Air Travel bookings for domestic travel within United States. 

Objective:
1. Predict a Price Range for Tickets based on the Origin Destination Combination for top 5 routes which have highest number of travel bookings.
2. An Optimal Price Range should be recommended for each travel month for each of the 5 routes.
3. Recommend Rules to be set in Travel Booking Portal , so that alerts can be configured when tickets exceeding range are booked.
